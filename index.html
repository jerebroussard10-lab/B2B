How These Numbers Are Calculated
Formula reference — for managers & back-end staff
Daily Ceiling (Allowed Max)
(Budget − Prior Days' Actuals) ÷ Days Remaining incl. Today
What you're allowed to spend today. Resets each morning based on how prior days went.
—
Over / Under Today
Today's Actual Hours − Daily Ceiling
Positive = over your ceiling (compresses future days). Negative = you have buffer.
—
Allowed Tomorrow (Avg)
(Budget − All Actuals Through Today) ÷ Days After Today
Once today locks in, this spreads the remaining budget across future days only.
—
FTE Tomorrow
Allowed Tomorrow ÷ Hrs Per Shift (12)
Translates tomorrow's hour ceiling into an FTE headcount target for scheduling.
—
Daily Utilization %
(Today's Actual ÷ Daily Ceiling) × 100
100% = exactly on pace. Over 100% = compressing future budget. Under = earning buffer.
—
Alert Thresholds
Green ≤100% · Yellow 101–110% · Red >110%
Applied to daily utilization. Red = immediate staffing review required before next shift.
